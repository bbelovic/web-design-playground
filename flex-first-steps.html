Williams-Sonoma is a home products and furnishings retailer with a market cap of $14 billion.
These furnishings are provided across a range of branded retailers that include Pottery Barn, West Elm, and the eponymous Williams-Sonoma.
Well, the business is just as high quality as its furnishings are, thanks in no small part to the fact that they carry no long-term debt whatsoever.
