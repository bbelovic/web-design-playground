Have your web resources under control.

This fast age leads to lots of browser tabs opened with many interesting articles, blogs, stories and other resources. Many of them left opened without me returned to them ever after. I always thought I will just return to it later - but later equals never. Tabs waited opened forever for me to get back to them, once upon a time restarted browser or computer closing all tabs and started with clean browser window. Until I gather another group of tabs, restarted computer, lost all the interesting websites and repeating this over and over again.

Being frustrated with this I decided to do something about it. I started developing application which will save URLs to pages opened in browser tabs. This allowed me to return to any of these pages anytime in the future.
